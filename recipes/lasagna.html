An unconventional way of baking your lasagna at home

A delicious twist on the classic Italian dish, featuring layers of tender pasta sheets, a creamy white sauce, and a medley of seafood such as shrimp, crab, and scallops, all baked to perfection with a golden, cheesy crust.

Ingredients for untraditional lasagna

  • shrimp
  • crab meat
  • scallops
  • lasagna noodles
  • white sauce (béchamel)
  • mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • garlic
  • onion

Step-by-step how to make an untraditional lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic in a bit of olive oil until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the shrimp, crab meat, and scallops to the skillet and cook until they are just opaque, about 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. In a separate saucepan, prepare the white sauce (béchamel) by melting butter, whisking in flour to create a roux, and then slowly adding milk while whisking until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the white sauce on the bottom. Then layer with noodles, more white sauce, the seafood mixture, and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of white sauce and a generous topping of cheese.
  7. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
