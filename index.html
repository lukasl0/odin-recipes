Lasagna recipe Home

a classic Italian baked dish featuring alternating layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, savory sauces (typically meat ragù and béchamel), and cheeses like ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan.

  • ground beef
  • marinara sauce
  • lasagna noodles
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • onion
  • garlic

Step-by-step how to make a lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the marinara sauce and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with a beaten egg and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  7. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. Then layer with noodles, more meat sauce, dollops of the ricotta mixture, and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella cheese.
