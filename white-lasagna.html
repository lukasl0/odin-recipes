White lasagna recipe

Traditional Lasagna
  • lasagna noodles
  • chicken breast
  • Alfredo sauce
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • onion
  • garlic

Step-by-step how to make a white lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the chicken breast over medium heat until fully cooked. Remove from the skillet and shred or dice the chicken.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the Alfredo sauce and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with a beaten egg and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  7. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the Alfredo sauce on the bottom. Then layer with noodles, more Alfredo sauce, shredded chicken, dollops of the ricotta mixture, and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of Alfredo sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella cheese.
